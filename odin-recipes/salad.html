Salad Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 cups mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/4 cup olives (black or green)
  • 1/4 cup nuts (walnuts or almonds)
  • Your choice of salad dressing (vinaigrette, ranch, etc.)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, and red onion.
  2. Add diced avocado, feta cheese, olives, and nuts to the salad.
  3. Drizzle your choice of salad dressing over the top.
  4. Toss gently to combine all ingredients.
  5. Serve immediately or refrigerate for up to 30 minutes before serving.
  6. Garnish with additional feta cheese and nuts if desired.

Serving Suggestions

Serve as a side dish or add grilled chicken or shrimp for a complete meal.
