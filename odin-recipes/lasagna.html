Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add marinara sauce, salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning to the skillet. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the mozzarella cheese.
  6. Spread a layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  7. Layer 3 lasagna noodles, followed by half of the ricotta mixture, half of the remaining meat sauce, and a third of the mozzarella cheese.
  8. Repeat layers, finishing with noodles and remaining meat sauce on top.
  9. Sprinkle with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  10. Bake for 30-35 minutes until bubbly and golden brown.
  11. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil.

Serving Suggestions

Serve with garlic bread and a side salad for a complete meal.

Tips

  • For a vegetarian version, substitute ground beef with sautéed vegetables.
  • Make it ahead of time and refrigerate overnight. Bake when ready to serve.
  • Experiment with different cheeses for a unique flavor.
  • Pair with a glass of red wine for a delightful dining experience.

Storage

Store leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat in the oven or microwave before serving.

Conclusion
